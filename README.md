# E-Commerce Customer Segmentation: K-Means Clustering

This repository contains a data pipeline that processes high-volume transaction data to identify profitable customer cohorts and optimize marketing spend.

## The Business Problem
Broad-spectrum ad spend wastes capital on low-intent users. The business required a deterministic model to identify high-value customer personas and automate targeted campaigns.

## The Architecture
* Analyzed over 500,000 e-commerce transactions.
* Engineered features around purchase frequency, average order value, and recency.
* Deployed a K-Means clustering algorithm to segment 4,000+ unique customers into four actionable behavioral personas.

## The Impact
The segmentation model allowed the marketing team to reallocate ad spend dynamically, targeting high-conversion cohorts and significantly reducing customer acquisition cost (CAC) on low-intent traffic.
